Appendix E — How to read this report

Owner-reported
Every entry was logged by the owner on a phone. This is a record of what the owner observed, not a clinical examination, and contains no diagnosis or treatment recommendation.
Denominators
Counts are shown over their window and the days logged (e.g. “9 episodes / 52 d”), so a count is never read without knowing how long and how completely it was tracked.
Logging coverage
Something was logged on 48 of 52 days; nothing was logged on May 13, May 27, Jun 11, or Jun 18. Absence of an entry is not evidence a symptom did not occur.
Meal completion
Counted from logged meals only; treats and human food are excluded. Unobserved or free-fed intake, when present, is reported as “intake not directly observed” and never counted as a meal refused.
Severity
Owner-reported on a 1–5 scale, per event in appendix A. It is intentionally never averaged into a headline figure; trend is read from frequency.
Time confidence
seen the owner witnessed it (exact time) · est an estimated time · range found later; the window it occurred in is shown, not the time it was noticed.
Associations
Any timing relationship is reported as co-occurrence with counts for the clinician to weigh. Nothing in this report asserts that a food caused a symptom.
Deleted entries
Entries the owner deleted are excluded. Every figure in this report is computed over exactly the events listed in appendices A–B — nothing is counted that is not shown.
Range
Scoped to since the last veterinary visit (May 1, 2026). The owner can change the range.
