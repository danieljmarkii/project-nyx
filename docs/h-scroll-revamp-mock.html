Killing the silent horizontal scroll

The complaint: option rows that scroll sideways with no visible edge or indicator. Users don't realise there's more, so they pick from only what they can see. Worst on single-select pickers — Medication Form (10 options), Route (8), and Food Format (10) — where a missed option means wrong data.

Low-fi. One example shown (Medication · Form). The same fix applies to Route and Food Format.  Nyx tokens, real option lists.
