The widget's jobs — PM-defined ROUND 3 SPINE

The widget will never be as robust as the app. It has exactly two jobs.
  • Job 1 — keep Culprit top of mind so logging happens, so we can help pets. The status column is the ambient half; the big tiles are the capture half.
  • Job 2 — "when in doubt, app it out." Every path that can't produce a named event in ≤2 taps opens the app instead. The dashed "Something else…" row is this job made visible — it appears in every picker, always last, always honest.
