Surface 1 of 5

Home — three zones become one narrative

  • Signal unchanged in role, upgraded in connection: the lead card now ends in an evidence row — “See the pattern →” — that opens the chart behind the sentence. Cards finally earn Principle 3's “tappable to reveal the evidence.”
  • Today is now the day's story: a time-axis strip (meals solid, treats hollow, symptoms clay) + one narrative line, instead of “last 3 events + N more.” Built for the real shape: ~8 feedings/day. Tap → today in History.
  • Trend zone → Pattern card: bigger bars, a feeding lane for context, and the whole card is tappable into a 30-day explorer. This resolves B-067 by making the chart the evidence layer: reflection ③ suppresses when the Pattern card charts that symptom, and both share one window helper.
  • Safety invariant intact: the safety card always leads, is never capped, and the rose accent is now reserved for the safety lane — everything observational is clay.
  • Stretch flag: Principle 3's zone list (Signal/Today/Trend) is reinterpreted as Signal/Today/Pattern with shared evidence — needs a Tier-2 doc note.

Surface 2 of 5

Pattern explorer — the chart you can finally touch

  • Tap-through from Home or the Signal card. 14/30/all-time ranges, per-day bars, tap a bar → that day's detail (episode time, timing-to-meal when witnessed, what else was logged) with a jump into History.
  • Two lanes, deliberately separated: symptom episodes (clay) and meal-logging coverage (mint). The Data Scientist's line: shown together for context, never drawn as one correlated chart — the footnote says exactly that, and signed claims stay in the Signal.
  • Windows match the engine: “this week / last week” here uses the same rolling-7-day helper as detectors ③/④, killing B-067's contradictory-numbers risk by construction.
  • Never reassures: a quiet chart gets no “all clear” copy — empty days are just empty bars.
Surface 3 of 5

History — days, not a wall

  • Day-grouped by default. At 8.4 events/day, the chronological dump was ~50 rows per screen-week. A day card = date + count pills; symptom rows always visible even collapsed (the 16 vomit events are the needles; the 228 meals are the haystack); feedings collapse to one summary row (“3 meals · 4 treats · all eaten fully”).
  • Density control: Days ⇄ All-events toggle. Filters and pull-to-refresh unchanged; type-filtering “Vomit” flips to the flat view automatically.
  • Time honesty carried through: found/estimated events keep their B-010 tags in the group (“~7:00 AM · estimated”).
  • Free-feeding strip and boundary markers stay — the strip stays pinned; markers render at their day's foot.
  • Intake at a glance: the rating rides each row as a quiet chip; a refusal renders clay, not red, and is just a fact on the row.
Surface 4 of 5

Foods — a top-level tab (B-004), photo-forward

  • Fourth tab. The library stopped being a picker detail: 46 foods, 18 brands, free-feeding management, and now an intake record per food. Browsing without logging is a real job.
  • Photo-forward tiles, without the chaos: 42/46 foods already have front-of-pack photos. The fix for the abandoned photo-grid attempt is an AI pack-shot crop: extraction returns a crop box, the client crops once at add-time (no new server image dependency), tiles get uniform clean pack art. Text tile remains the no-photo fallback.
  • “Reliable favorites” obeys the intake invariant: positive-only, floor of 5+ rated offers at ≥90% eaten-fully, denominator printed on the card (“26 of 26”). No dislike shelf exists — decline is a health signal and routes to detector ②, never to a taste verdict.
  • Stretch flag: “three tabs maximum at MVP” (interaction principles) becomes four. Argued as: MVP qualifier + the FAB remains the universal log path, so navigation stays flat.
  • Brand canonicalization needed (“Purina Friskies” vs “Friskies” are two brands today) before brand grouping ships — composes with B-052.
Surface 4b

Food detail — the intake record

  • Rate over N, denominator first: “Eaten fully 16 of 18 rated offers · offered 24 times · 6 unrated.” The stacked bar shows full/partial/refused as counts, never a star rating, never the word “picky.”
  • Refusals route to health, in the copy itself: the footnote under every intake record says refusals are watched by the Signal as an appetite question — the surface never interprets them as taste.
  • Everything else stays: always-available toggle (B-040), editable ingredients, re-run extraction.
Surface 5 of 5

FAB — a sheet, three jobs, nothing else

  • Bottom sheet replaces the popover — thumb-reach, 44pt+ rows, same one-tap re-log (kept, as ordered: it's the best thing about the FAB). Recent items show pack-art thumbnails and treats are eligible, not just meals.
  • “Log with photo” is gone. Photos attach inside every flow; a photo is evidence on an event, not an event type.
  • Symptom chips stay (vomit / loose stool, clay not red), then one “Something else” row into the full log screen. Five decisions became three.
