The rest of the spec

§8.1 Coverage ≠ wellness

"Show all logging" is off by default. On, a faint neutral tick marks any day with ≥1 event of any type. A bare day means nothing was logged — never read as "the pet is well".

cut Six-month strip

The cumulative strip is gone (you called it low-value). Its "how many months has this run?" read moves to the vet report as a "symptom months" line — the clinician's surface.

a11y VoiceOver

Every cell carries a full label — "Jun 24, 4 vomiting episodes" — so the count is spoken, not just shown. Summary and empty states read too.

hold Capture untouched

Zero change to logging, History, or the quick-log flow (register rule §1.2 — records stay the shipped light system). This PR is presentation of existing data only.
