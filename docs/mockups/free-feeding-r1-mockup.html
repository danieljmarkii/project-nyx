What this mockup is asserting (and what it deliberately isn't)

  • Set once, not logged. The whole interaction is a single toggle — zero additions to the 10-second quick-log path (Principle 1).
  • Two states shown. Screen 1 is the ON confirmation; the OFF state is just the same card, plain border, toggle left, no helper/caveat.
  • Attribution-honest copy. Single-cat → “definitely her.” The shared-bowl / multi-cat low-attribution case is not here — that's the multi-pet sprint (is_shared hook reserved inert).
  • No quantity anywhere. A standing fact has no per-meal amount — refill logs, photo plate-waste, % consumed are all deferred (§0).
  • Never reassures. Every surface that touches free-fed food carries “intake not directly observed” / “quiet days ≠ didn't eat.”
  • Vet line is a superset of WSAVA. Feeding-method as a first-class field; renders when Step 9 lands (R1 ships the data).
Open at-build decisions (spec §7): exact entry-point placement (food-detail toggle is the lean pick shown here vs pet-profile-first), whether to also capture meal_fed or infer it from logged meals, and active-window editing depth. None block the mockup.
