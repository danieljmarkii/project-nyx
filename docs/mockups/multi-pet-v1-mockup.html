What this mock is settling

  • A3 vs A4 — the safety breakthrough (PM call). The banner is the team's lean: it renders only for safety-class findings on the non-active pet, sits above the Signal because it belongs to a different pet, and is one tap from the right home. A4 shows the cost of leaving it out — a calm screen over a live intake flag on the other cat. It is new Principle-3 surface area, which is why it needs your ratification, not ours.
  • B2 vs B3 — free-feeding capture (PM call). Both keep free-feeding in the food domain (your 2026-06-09 "food belongs with food" decision intact — the pet page stays untouched). B2 shows everything and makes shared bowls self-evident; B3 is smaller but hides the other pet's arrangement. Data model is identical either way: two per-pet rows in feeding_arrangements, zero schema or engine work; the future household-bowl consolidation stays the reserved is_shared hook.
  • Decided, shown for confirmation: per-pet home (A1), device-local switcher with "Add a pet" in the sheet (A2), pet identity leading the log sheet with a pre-log flip and no move-to-pet (B1), archive-only removal (B4).
  • Voice notes: banner copy is specific and calm ("hasn't finished a meal since Tuesday — worth a look"), never alarm language; archive copy is warm and honest about reversibility; free-feeding keeps the "quiet days ≠ didn't eat" promise.
  • Open for PM on this page: ① A3 or A4 — banner in or out? ② B2 or B3 — per-pet rows or active-pet toggle? ③ Switcher sheet contents (A2) — anything missing or extra? ④ B1's "Logging for Pixel" placement — leading the sheet, or smaller/quieter?
